## Sensors — What We Actually Measure
- **Substrate sensors** measure: VWC (volumetric water content) and EC-porewater (electrical conductivity of the porewater)
- These two measurements (VWC and ecPW) are the atomic value of irrigation decisions — everything about how and when to irrigate derives from them
- **Sensors do NOT require calibration and do NOT drift**
- What Addium did: validated that VWC and ecPW curves do not change over time, that sensors stay within spec, and that the numbers are real and stay accurate and precise across the entire volumetric curve
- This is a key differentiator — the data is trustworthy at the hardware level before it ever reaches software
